My Top 3 Favorite episodes of Adventure Time!

  1. Season 6 Ep.6 "The Citadel"
  2. Season 4 Ep.18 "King Worm"
  3. Season 5 Ep.15 "A Glitch is a Glitch"

Plot Overview for each Episode

  1. In the episode "The Citadel", Finn and Jake discover that Finn's dad is trapped in a place called the citadel and to free him they must commit a cosmic crime. When they get there Finn's dad tries to escapse causing Finn to lose an arm.
  2. In the episode "King Worm", Finn and Jake are trapped in a dream world and throughout the episode they figure out how to get out as they realize they were trapped there by the King Worm and deprieve it of food.
  3. This is a special episode that animated entirly in 3D. Finn and Jake need to stop Ice King from destroying the world. The credits music in this episode is made by indie musician, Flying Lotus.
